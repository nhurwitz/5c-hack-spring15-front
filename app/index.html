About Wikipath

Wikipath finds the shortest path form one Wikipedia page to another. This can be useful for high scores in the Wikipedia Game or just for exploring the vastness of Wikipedia.

It is built and maintained by Noam Hurwtiz, Dima Smirnov, Ziv Epstein and Ted Kornish and was originally implemented for the Claremont 5C Hackthon where it won first prize. We hope you like it!
